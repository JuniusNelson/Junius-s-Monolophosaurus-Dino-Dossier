About Dinosaurs

Dinosaurs are a diverse group of reptiles that once roamed the Earth. They lived during the Mesozoic Era, and they come in various shapes and sizes.

Dinosaur Types

There were two main types of dinosaurs: Saurischia (lizard-hipped) and Ornithischia (bird-hipped). The Saurischia group includes the famous T. rex, while the Ornithischia group contains dinosaurs like the Triceratops.

Fossil Discoveries

Fossils of dinosaurs have been discovered on every continent. These ancient remnants help us understand the history and biology of these incredible creatures.
